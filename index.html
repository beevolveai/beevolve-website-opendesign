The process

Four weeks, four steps. No platform to learn.

Every engagement runs the same way — predictable, documented, and built to outlast us. You own the system at the end. We just leave you with one.

i

Audit

We sit with your team for a week, shadow the work, and map every repetitive task to time and dollars. You get a workflow diagram and a ranked opportunity list.

Week 1 · On-site or remote
ii

Design

You see the system architecture before a line of code is written. Tools, integrations, hand-off points, failure modes — all on paper, all approved by you.

Week 1–2 · Async approvals
iii

Build

Our engineers ship the automation in three weeks on average. You get the source, the docs, the credentials, and a Loom for every moving part.

Week 2–4 · Daily updates
iv

Hand-off

We train your team, monitor the system for 30 days, and step away. No retainers, no lock-in. The hive keeps running because it was built to.

Week 4+ · 30-day warranty
