Transparent pricing

Priced by the build,
not the seat.

No retainers. No lock-in. No monthly seat fees. You get a fixed-scope quote after a free 30-minute discovery call — and you keep everything we build.

Why no public price list?

Automation is bespoke.
Pricing should be too.

Every workflow is different

A 3-step Zapier replacement and a full AI-agent CRM rebuild aren't the same problem. Quoting a single number for "automation" would mean overcharging simple jobs or under-scoping complex ones.

We quote scope, not time

We scope the outcome you need — "leads enriched and routed in under 60 seconds" — and price that. Not "10 developer hours." You know exactly what you're getting before we start.

No surprises at the end

Our quotes are fixed. If the build takes longer, that's on us. You're not paying hourly for scope creep we didn't catch in discovery.

The audit is free

We spend 30 minutes mapping your workflows with you. If we can't find 10+ hours to give you back, we tell you so — and you keep the process diagram we drew. No commitment required.
